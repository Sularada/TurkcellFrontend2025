Frequently Asked Questions

How do holographic products work?

Our products use advanced optical projection and AI technology to create interactive, three-dimensional visuals that blend seamlessly with the real world.

How much space do the products take up?

HoloWorld products are designed to be compact and modern. For instance, the HoloTale Projector is about the size of a small speaker, and the HoloStory Book is similar to a tablet in size.

Do I need special equipment to use these products?

No additional equipment is needed. Our products are standalone devices. For advanced features, you can connect them to Wi-Fi or use our dedicated mobile app for seamless integration.

Can the products be personalized?

Absolutely! Many of our holographic devices can be customized to match your preferences. For instance, the HoloLegend Simulator lets you create your own character and storyline.

How can I order your products? Is it secure?

You can purchase our products directly through our website using secure payment methods. Digital products are instantly activated, while physical items will be delivered to your doorstep.

Can I get technical support for the products?

Of course! Our support team is available to assist you before and after your purchase. Simply visit the Customer Support section on our website to get in touch with us.
