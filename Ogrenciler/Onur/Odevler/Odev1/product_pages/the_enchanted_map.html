The Enchanted Map

Price: Your free will 🎭

Uncover the secrets of your future and navigate directly to your ultimate success with The Enchanted Map of Destiny. This magical map knows your heart’s deepest desires and shows you the fastest, most direct path to achieving them. Whether it’s wealth, love, or power, the map leads you there with unerring precision. But caution is key… Each time you follow the map’s guidance, you lose the ability to make your own choices, becoming a puppet of fate. Over time, you’ll no longer remember why you wanted your desires in the first place. You’ll reach your destination, but the journey—and your free will—will be lost forever. Luckily, someone like Rick might tweak it, giving you control over the map’s powers without losing your autonomy!
